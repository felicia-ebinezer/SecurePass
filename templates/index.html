Three steps to safety

A simple process designed to give you maximum insight with minimum effort.

1

Type or paste your password into the secure input field. It's never stored in plain text — only a SHA-256 hash is used.

2

Instant Analysis

Our engine evaluates character diversity, length, special characters, and checks against a local database of previously used password hashes.

Get Results & Recommendations
